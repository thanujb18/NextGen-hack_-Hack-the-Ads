Problem Statement: 
                   People often click on links without knowing if they are safe, leading to scams and data theft.
Malicious URLs can look very real, and existing tools are often too complex or unclear.

Solution:
          In today’s digital world, people interact with links almost every day—through messages, emails, and social media.
Most of the time, users click on these links without thinking twice about their safety.
However, many of these links can be malicious and are designed to trick users into sharing sensitive information.
They often look very similar to legitimate websites, making them difficult to identify.
Existing tools that detect such threats can be confusing or too technical for regular users.
Because of this, many people remain unaware of the risks they are exposed to.
Our project aims to solve this problem by creating a simple and user-friendly website.
It allows users to enter any URL and quickly check whether it is safe or potentially harmful.
The system not only gives a result but also provides a score to indicate the level of risk.
Additionally, it explains the reasons behind the result, helping users make smarter and safer decisions online.
